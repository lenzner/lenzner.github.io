About me

I am a macroecologist and terrestrial ecologist with a focus on global change ecology, invasion biology and biodiversity research. I am particularly interested in large-scale patterns of diversity and how environmental, socio-economic and societal drivers drivers interact from a systemic point of view. See my CV here.

Current Work

Global Naturalized Alien Floras database (GloNAF)

Investigating macroecological and macroevolutionary patterns of plant invasions.
Official webpage

Alien Scenarios

Developing and applying scenarios and models of biological invasions for the 21st century.
Official webpage

IPBES - Invasive Species Assessment

Assessment of current status and trends of invasive alien species, their impacts, their drivers, their management, and options for policy to deal with the challenges they pose.
